# 📊 Apex Presence Insights Dashboard

An interactive **Power BI dashboard** designed to analyze employee attendance patterns, work-from-home trends, and leave utilization. This dashboard enables HR teams and managers to monitor workforce presence, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Apex Presence Insights Dashboard** provides a comprehensive view of employee attendance by tracking key HR metrics such as attendance percentage, work-from-home percentage, sick leave percentage, and total present days.

The dashboard transforms raw attendance data into meaningful business insights through interactive visualizations and KPI cards.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Organizations often struggle to monitor workforce attendance efficiently due to scattered or unorganized data.

This dashboard helps answer questions like:

- What is the overall attendance percentage?
- How many employees are working from home?
- What is the sick leave trend?
- Which days have the highest attendance?
- How does attendance vary across weeks and months?
